Project Information

Due to the bad quality of my current Internet Service Provider, I decided to measure the stability of the Internet connection I'm subscribed to in order to find out what's the best time to work at home. I've used an old Raspberry Pi model B to take periodic speed tests (using this library), then shed some light regarding this ISP's quality. The RaspberryPi is connected via Ethernet to the DSL router.
